🃏 ABILITIES
Draw these from Ability tiles or Choice tiles. Use wisely — they can flip the game!
Mobility 🐥
Warp Wings
Your next dice roll counts DOUBLE. Land twice as far on the board!
💡 Best used to sprint past a dangerous tile or race to GO for +1 HP.
Lifesteal 💧
Quack Drain
Steal 2 HP from a chosen opponent immediately.
💡 Deadly on a low-HP target. Can trigger an elimination at the right moment.
Escape 🍃
Feather Dodge
Your next Combat tile is completely ignored.
💡 Save it when you're low and can't afford to lose a duel.
Heal 💚
Healing Splash
Instantly restore 5 HP! With low HP modes, that's nearly half your health.
💡 The most powerful card in Blitz mode (8 HP total). Use ASAP if critical.
Defense 🛁
Bubble Armor
The next hit you receive is completely blocked—zero damage, no matter the amount.
💡 Use before entering a Combat tile you can't avoid.
Teleport 🌀
Quack-Port
Warp instantly to Tile 0 (GO!) and earn +1 HP as a bonus for the trip.
💡 Great escape hatch when you're deep in enemy territory with bad tiles ahead.
Control ❄️
Freeze Tag
Target player skips their entire next turn.
💡 Use on a dangerous player right before they'd land on an ability or combat tile.
Counter 🪞
Mirror Shield
The next attack aimed at you is reflected BACK at the attacker instead.
💡 Equip when you suspect someone will target you with a Hard/Extreme combo.
