# 🦆 Ducking Around

A logic-based strategy board game where **DUCK HEROES** compete to prove who is the ultimate champion of the pond!

## ⚠️ IMPORTANT DISCLAIMER
**DO NOT use the Multiplayer mode (yet... hopefully launching that by tomorrow).** The online sync features are very, very bugged and may lead to unexpected behavior. 
* **For the best experience:** Play against an **AI opponent** on **Endurance Mode**.
* **Alternatively:** Play against yourself (or multi-human on one device) for maximum chaos! >:D

## 📖 LORE
In the world of **DUCKING AROUND**, there can only be one true Duck Hero. The legendary **DUCK SQUAD** (the Square of Opposition ducks) is a family of four philosophical ducks that the **DUCK HEROES** exploit and manipulate to fight each other... only if they use them correctly. By mastering the logic of their relationships, you can deal massive damage and outmaneuver your rivals.

## 🎮 HOW TO PLAY?
Don't forget to read through the **RULES** and **DUCKS** section before starting a game!
1.  **Setup:** Choose the number of players, add AI opponents, and select your Game Mode (Standard, Blitz, or Endurance).
2.  **Movement:** Roll the dice and move your duck around the 40-tile board.
3.  **Tiles:**
    * **Combat:** Challenge an opponent to a duel. Higher difficulty deals more damage!
    * **Logic:** Answer logic questions based on the Square of Opposition to move forward or avoid damage.
    * **Ability/Choice:** Draw powerful Ability Cards! (In Choice, you can heal HP and answer a logic question for benefits/penalties too!)
4.  **The Corners:**
    * **GO!:** Pass to restore 1 HP.
    * **Duck Jail:** Roll doubles to escape!
    * **Quack For All:** A high-stakes logic challenge for everyone.
5.  **Winning:** Reduce all other ducks to 0 HP. Last duck standing wins!
6.  **HAVE FUN!** With much love, Group 7 of SS10. <3
